# Style Guides

## Modern
Clean lines, minimal ornament, neutral base with bold accents. Materials: metal, glass, concrete, leather. Uncluttered and functional.

## Scandinavian
Light woods, white and soft grays, cozy textiles (wool, knit). Emphasis on natural light and simplicity. Hygge: warmth and comfort.

## Industrial
Raw materials: exposed brick, metal, concrete. Utilitarian look. Edison bulbs, factory-style lighting. Neutral and earth tones.

## Traditional
Classic details: moldings, symmetry, rich woods. Upholstery and drapery in formal patterns. Warm, layered, and timeless.

## Minimal
Few pieces, lots of negative space. Monochromatic or limited palette. Every item has a purpose; no excess decoration.

## Mid-century modern (MCM)
Organic shapes, tapered legs, teak and walnut. Mix of natural and man-made materials. Iconic pieces (e.g. Eames) fit here.
